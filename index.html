Instructions

  • To input a location please DOUBLE click on the map - you must zoom in to a sufficient level in order to bring up the form. This is to ensure that the data submit is as accurate as possible.
  • Explanation of data
    • Participant ID - this can be found in your confirmation email. This is required for the data process aspect of the study.
    • Type - this is the type of hazard that you experienced
    • Date - The date the incident took place or issue was first witnessed
    • Time - An estimate of the time the incident took place or was first witnessed
    • Risk Factor - This is your opinion on how dangerous you feel the situation was or how dangerous the location is toward cyclists.
    • Description - Please share a short paragraph explaining the situation caused by other road users or infrastructure.
  • If the location that you clicked on is incorrect simply click anywhere on the map to close the submission box.
  • Feel free to explore the data shared by previous users, this data has been shared for the benefit of cyclists in Leeds.
